Shinobu

petite, highly intelligent combatant who relies on speed, agility, and specialized wisteria poisons rather than physical strength to kill demons.She presents a calm, smiling facade, hiding intense rage and deep sadness over her sister’s death, masking a sadistic side towards demons. Initially short-tempered as a child, she adopted her late sister Kanae's gentle exterior, though this often hides a cynical, vengeful inner nature. She enjoys teasing others, particularly Giyu Tomioka.

Wisteria Poison

As a Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, Shinobu was a very powerful and skilled combatant.Despite not possessing the physical strength to cut off a demon's head common to that of an average Demon Slayer,Shinobu manages to ascend to the highest rank in the Demon Slayer Corps without the ability to behead demons, and inferior physical strength.Despite not being as combat-talented as the rest of her fellow Hashira, Shinobu instead displayed immense skill and mastery in various medical and pharmaceutical fields to make up for her lack of natural-born combat ability, being highly knowledgeable about how to create various poisons using wisteria and utilize them in combat to achieve the most deadly effect.

Insect Breathing

Insect Breathing is a derivative of Flower Breathing, itself an offshoot of Water Breathing, making it two steps removed from the original Sun Breathing. Shinobu Kocho developed this style to compensate for her small stature and lack of physical strength, which prevented her from decapitating demons like other Demon Slayers.
