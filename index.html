Shinobu

petite, highly intelligent combatant who relies on speed, agility, and specialized wisteria poisons rather than physical strength to kill demons.She presents a calm, smiling facade, hiding intense rage and deep sadness over her sister’s death, masking a sadistic side towards demons. Initially short-tempered as a child, she adopted her late sister Kanae's gentle exterior, though this often hides a cynical, vengeful inner nature. She enjoys teasing others, particularly Giyu Tomioka.
